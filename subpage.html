About Tifco Hotel Group

Tifco Hotel Group is the second largest Hotel Operator in Ireland.

We own and manage an exclusive portfolio of internationally branded hotels along with a variety of unique private label hotels. We have 24 hotel properties and almost 3,000 rooms in total.

We employ almost 1500 team members.

Our Hotels are located in Dublin and other key locations throughout Ireland.
