The Board

Enda O'Meara Chief Executive Officer

Enda started in the hotel industry in 1982, graduating with honours from Shannon College of Hotel Management. Having worked for major hotels in the UK, Europe and North America in 1997 Enda opened the founding hotel of the Tifco Hotel Group; Clontarf Castle Hotel . Enda has been instrumental in the development and expansion of Tifco Hotel Group into what it is today, one of Ireland's largest hotel companies. He has been actively involved in the construction of new hotels, along with the acquisition and disposal of other Hotels.

Brian Campion Chief Financial Officer

Brian is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants and has a wide portfolio of experience working in industry for the last 25 years. He has worked exclusively in the hospitality sector for the last 15 years for a number of well-known Irish groups and has gained extensive experience of the industry having been involved in a number of sizeable acquisitions, disposals and new openings.
