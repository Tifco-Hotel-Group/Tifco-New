Grow With Tifco

Alan Rooney

My name is Alan Rooney and my current position is Deputy General Manager at Hilton Dublin Kilmainham.

I first joined Tifco Hotel Group in August 2007 as part of the opening team in Crowne Plaza Dundalk. At this time I was beginning year 4 of my Hospitality Management degree. I always knew I was going to be a hotel general manager, and after doing some background research on the company behind the opening of this new hotel in Dundalk, I was sure that Tifco Hotel Group could present me with the perfect opportunity to realise my ambition.

I joined the team as Food & Beverage Assistant in the hotel bar. From here I held further positions such as Conference & Banqueting Porter, Conference & Banquet Supervisor, Food & Beverage Supervisor, Duty Manager and Wedding & Events Co-ordinator. After 5 years at this property, an opportunity arose in Crowne Plaza Dublin - Blanchardstown. I applied for the position of Conference & Banquet Manager, and took up the position there in 2012. I was responsible for the day to day planning and operation of 12 busy meeting rooms with banqueting for up to 450 and conference facilities for up to 650. During my 4 years here, I also held the positions of Food & Beverage Manager and then finally Senior Manager. In April 2016, my next opportunity arose and I took up my current position as Deputy General Manager in Hilton Dublin Kilmainham.

What I like most about working in hospitality is the constant need to grow and evolve. It's true that no two days are the same, each day brings a new challenge and each challenge brings a new opportunity. In my experience, creating opportunities is something that Tifco Hotel Group excels at. The roles are challenging, but the personal reward is great. Growing with the company and having the opportunity to upskill at every corner is something that isn't easily found. There is a real emphasis on growing your own talent here, seeing a spark in somebody, helping them to realise that potential, and, ultimately, helping them to become future managers. A career in hospitality is rewarding, and the opportunities many. I'm definitely looking forward to seeing what the future has in store for both myself and Tifco Hotel Group.

Stephan Ernest

My name is Stephan Ernest and I am currently working as Deputy General Manager for the Award winning Crowne Plaza Hotel Dublin Airport as well as the Holiday Inn Express Dublin Airport. This year will be my 9th year with the Tifco Hotel Group, whom I joined in September 2008.

My hospitality education started early! I commenced my hospitality studies in Rotterdam, when I turned 15 years old and finished my Bachelor Degree in International Hospitality Management in Breda 6 years later.

I moved to Dublin straight after and started my professional career as a Junior Duty Manager in The Burlington Hotel. I spent 2 years working there, assisting the various departments, learning about daily operations, taking in the exciting hotel atmosphere and I loved every day.

When the Burlington hotel closed, I was given the opportunity to start my employment with The Crowne Plaza Hotel and Holiday Inn Express Dublin Airport as Assistant Conference & Banqueting Manager and I have not looked back since!

I remember the first few months in particular to be challenging for me. The learning curve was a steep one as it tends to be for most team members joining, but I found that the clients whom I looked after were pleased with my efforts and this gave me the confidence I needed to establish myself.

3 years after joining, I was promoted to the position of Restaurant Manager and shortly thereafter, I was asked to take up the role of Conference & Banqueting Manager given my previous experience in the department. What I found most rewarding in those days was to get the team working towards a common goal and the instant gratification of reaching that goal together.

I do sometimes wonder where I would be if I would have chosen a different path. Although it is impossible to answer this question, I truly believe that my time here has equipped me to successfully fulfil my role as Deputy General Manager and I look forward to many more years within the organization.
