Management Team

David Hennessy Group General Manager (Travelodge Ireland)

David has worked in the hotel industry for over 28 years, with senior management experience at both single and multi-unit level, across the international branded and independent 3, 4 and 5 star sectors in Ireland and the UK. A graduate of Shannon College of Hotel Management, joining Tifco in 2014 in the capacity of General Manager of Clontarf Castle Hotel, before moving into a Group General Manager role taking responsibility for the Management of the Travelodge Ireland Portfolio following its acquisition in December 2016

David Monks Development Director

David has over 25 years’ experience in the hotel industry and most recently held the post of General Manager of the 502-bedroom Doubletree by Hilton (The Burlington Hotel) from 2011-2016. In 2017 David moved to a development and construction role with a focus on the delivery of new hotel assets to the Tifco portfolio. David is a graduate of the Shannon College of Hotel Management and also holds a Bachelor of Commerce, a Masters in Real Estate as well as Diploma’s in Construction Law and Project Management.

Thomas Grehan Group Financial Controller

Thomas has 25 years’ experience working in the hotel industry. Thomas has worked in all areas of the business including operations and cost control before moving into Accounts 15 years ago. Thomas joined Tifco Hotel Group in 1998 and currently heads up the Finance Function for all our partner Hotels. Thomas is a member of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants.

David O’ Connell Group Marketing & Revenue Director

David has worked in the hotel industry for over 20 years and has held several senior sales positions in the Irish Hotel Industry. David is a Graduate of the Shannon College of Hotel Management and also holds Bachelor Degrees in Arts and Commerce and a Masters in Business Studies (Marketing) from the National University of Ireland - Galway. David joined Tifco Hotel Group in 2002 and now heads up the Marketing Department for the Group. David also manages the Group Revenue function. David also lectures part time in Marketing at Dublin City University.

Christoph Haustein European Sales Director

Christoph has worked in the hotel industry for over 25 years and has held many senior sales positions in both Germany and Ireland. Prior to joining Tifco Hotel Group in 2004, Christoph worked for many of the large International Hotel Groups including Kempinski and Accor. Christoph currently heads Tifco Hotel Group's European Sales eam for International Conferences and Events and Irish inbound Tour & Group Operators sales.

Wesley Bowden Head of Online

Wesley graduated from Griffith College with a degree in Computer Science and joined Tifco Hotel Group as group E-Commerce & Online Marketing Manager in 2010. Wesley has many years of experience in E-commerce and online marketing and has held senior positions in the E-Commerce departments for some of Europe's largest multinationals including Ryanair & Wyeth. Wesley currently heads up a team of 4 people who manage the online sales and e-marketing for the Group.

Tom Coleman Group Procurement Manager

Tom has held several senior Food & Beverage Management positions in the hotel industry in Ireland, England, France and Oman over the last 25 years. Tom joined Tifco hotel Group in 2000 and currently heads up the entire procurement department, which manages the procurement process for the hotels within the Group. Tom is a member of the Institute of Purchasing Managers.

Himanshu Sud Chief Technology Officer

Himanshu has worked in the hotel industry for over 12 years and has held several positions within the hotel industry. Himanshu is a Graduate of the Shannon College of Hotel Management and also holds a Masters Degree in Information Technology from DIT. Himanshu joined Tifco Hotel Group in 2003 and currently manages the IT department who manage the IT function for the Group.

Ciara Drohan Group Human Resources Manager

Ciara has worked in Human Resources roles for the last 20 years. She holds a Bachelor of Arts in HR Management from the National College of Ireland. Ciara joined the Tifco Hotel Group in 2021 and is a Chartered Member of CIPD and a member of the Irish Hospitality Institute.
