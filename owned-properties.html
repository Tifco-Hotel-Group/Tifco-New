Owned Properties

2008 - The Crowne Plaza Dublin-Blanchardstown, a 188 bedroom hotel and conference centre opened in 2008 at the Blanchardstown Centre, Dublin 15.

2015 - The Hilton Dublin Kilmainham acquisition of the 120 bedroom hotel located facing Kilmainham Gaol, only a mile from Heuston Railway Station was purchased by Tifco Hotel Group and subsequently sold to the current owners in 2018.

2016 - The Parliament Hotel (formerly Arlington Hotel Temple Bar), a 63 bedroom hotel in an ideal location in the heart of Medieval Dublin and overlooking historic Dublin Castle, The property subsequently underwent extensive refurbishment to become the first Hard Rock hotel in Ireland

2016 - Tifco Hotel Group purchased Travelodge Ireland , comprising 12 hotels throughout the island of Ireland.

2019 – Holiday Inn Express Dublin Airport – additional 100 bedrooms added to this Hotel

2020 - The Hard Rock Hotel Dublin; opened having converted the Parliament and adjacent office building into one of the best hotels in Dublin city centre. It is located on Lord Edward Street. Featuring 120 luxurious guest bedrooms and suites, furnished to an exceptionally high standard

2021 – Arthaus Hotel a unique Boutique Hotel located in the heart of Stephens Green, this 40 bedroom property exhibits a rich tapestry of art and design and was inspired by the world-famous Bauhaus school of art.

2022 – Travelodge Dublin City Centre opened in January 2022, with 393 high qualityl bedrooms in a central location close to all the key attractions in the city. It is one of the largest budget branded Hotels in Dublin city Centre.
