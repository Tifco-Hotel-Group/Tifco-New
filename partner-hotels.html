Partner Hotels

2014 - In May 2014 the 188-bedroom family focused Hotel Killarney was added as a partner Hotel to Tifco Hotel Group.

2017 - In March 2017 the 64 bedroom Innisfallen Hotel in Killarney was added to the Tifco Hotel Group portfolio. The Innisfallen Hotel is a family/leisure hotel set right in beautiful town of Killarne,y with many of Kerry’s greatest tourist attractions on its doorstep.

2018 - In November 2018 the 88 bedroom Kinsale Hotel & Spa was added to the Tifco Hotel Group portfolio. Set on 90 acres of mature wooded parkland and offering panoramic views over Oysterhaven Bay, the Kinsale Hotel & Spa is situated in the perfect location near Cork City, in a peaceful and tranquil setting for our guests.

2019 - In May 2019 the 147 bedroom Hendrick Smithfield opened as Ireland’s first street art hotel. The Hendrick’s art collection is particularly unique, celebrating the urban art-forms of graffiti and street art. Ireland’s finest artists practicing within this field hang side by side with many of the global greats.

The 92-bedroom Clybaun Hotel in Galway was added to the Tifco Hotel Group in June 2019 featuring conference capacity for 400 delegates, a fully featured fitness and leisure centre and beauty rooms.
